  <h2>⚠️ What we don't recommend</h2>
  <p>To stay on budget and learn the real fundamentals, avoid:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>$200/mo AI courses</strong> — most content is on YouTube for free</li>
    <li><strong>Proprietary agent platforms</strong> — they're renting you the future, you should own it</li>
    <li><strong>Cloud GPU services as a beginner</strong> — use free tiers first</li>
    <li><strong>Subscription LLM APIs without a budget</strong> — OpenRouter free models are surprisingly good</li>
  </ul>
