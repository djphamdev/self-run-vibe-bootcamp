  <h2>📈 When does paid make sense?</h2>
  <p>Free is the right call at the start. But there are moments worth
  paying:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Cursor Pro ($20/mo)</strong> — when free tier limits get in the way of shipping. Often month 3+.</li>
    <li><strong>OpenRouter credits ($5–$20/mo)</strong> — when free models are throttled and you need reliable access.</li>
    <li><strong>Better Hermes hardware</strong> — if your Pi is choking, $5–$10/mo VPS solves it.</li>
    <li><strong>Custom domain</strong> — $10/yr always worth it for a "real" project.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>Don't pay upfront. Pay when you feel the friction.</p>

  <h2>🎯 Our recommendation</h2>
  <p><strong>Start at $0.</strong> Stay at $0 for the first month. Add
  OpenRouter credits ($5) when you hit a wall. Add Cursor Pro ($20) when
  you ship your first project. By then you know what you actually need.</p>

  <p>The whole point of "vibe coding" is fast iteration. Spending hours
  comparing $20 IDEs is the opposite of vibe coding. Use Trae, use
  Windsurf, use Cursor's free tier, and keep building.</p>
